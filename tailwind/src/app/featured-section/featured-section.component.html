MAIN CHARACTERS

Jinx

Jinx, previously known as Powder, is one of the main characters in Arcane. She was an orphaned Zaunite after her parents were tragically slaughtered when she and her sister, Vi were younger.

Vi

Violet (commonly known as Vi) is a lead character appearing in Arcane. When her parents were killed by Piltover enforcers, she and her sister Powder were taken in by Vander.

Caitlyn

Caitlyn Kiramman is a major character appearing in Arcane. Eager to prove herself from under her parents' powerful shadow and overbearing nature, Caitlyn joined the Enforcers.

Jayce

Jayce is a lead character in Arcane. He is a scientist and innovator who, after years of success leading the city’s scientific and, consequently, its economic progress, became the Head of the Council of Piltover.

Viktor

Viktor, the future Machine Herald, is a research assistant who decides to throw in his lot with Jayce, who became his bestfriend, and his Hextech theory. As a result, both of them are responsible for the creation of Hextech and it's subsequent events.

Vander

Warwick (born Vander) was once the unspoken leader and protector of the undercity. Determined and zealous, Vander did all he could in his later life to ensure the safety of those under his protection—the residents of Zaun and, more specifically, the four children he'd taken under his wing.

Silco

ilco was a major supporting character in Season 1 of Arcane and second adopted father of Jinx. He was also the powerful, ambitious Zaunite leader of the chem-barons, silently controlling Zaun from under the noses of Piltover's authority.

Ambessa

Ambessa Medarda Was a powerful warlord and stateswoman in Noxus. A master of both eloquent speech and brutal force, she deftly manipulates situations and people to serve her and her family’s interests, whether through words or actions.

Ekko

Ekko is a major character appearing in Arcane. One of many children who grew up in the Lanes. Upon the death of his mentor and the disappearance of his friends, Ekko finds a way to survive on his own and help others fight back against Silco's reign over their home.

Heimerdinger

Heimerdinger is a renowned and respected figure in Piltover. A scientist, professor, dean, and once the head of the Council, Heimerdinger has helped mold the minds of young scholars at the Academy. He lived for over three centuries, hoping to guide and caution students from the potential risks of magic.

Singed

Singed, also known as the "Mad Chemist," is a brilliant yet enigmatic scientist in Arcane. Hailing from Zaun, he operates on the fringes of morality, pushing the boundaries of science regardless of the consequences making him both a genius and a harbinger of destruction.
